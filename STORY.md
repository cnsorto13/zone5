## Chapter 1 — The Blueprint
**April 2026**

Every app starts with a question. Zone 5 started with this one:

*Why do I have five tools for one training block?*

Strava for runs. A spreadsheet for planning. MyFitnessPal for nutrition. A notes app for how I felt. None of them talking to each other. A lot of data, very little intelligence.

So I decided to build the thing I actually wanted.

Before writing a single line of code, I spent a session doing what most people skip — thinking. What does the data model actually look like? What tables exist? What's required vs. optional? What decisions made now save pain later?

The answer was five tables. Runs. Lifts. Nutrition. Sleep. Weight. One platform. Three pillars. Full picture.

That's the foundation. Everything else gets built on top of it.
