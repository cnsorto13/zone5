# 12-Week Running Plan
**Apr 20 – Jul 12, 2026**
Phase 1 Recovery → Phase 2 Base → Phase 3 Marathon Specific

---

## How to Read This

- **Easy** — conversational pace, 145–152 HR. If it feels too easy, it's right.
- **Tempo** — format is: easy warmup → threshold block → easy cooldown. HR 162–168.
- **Long** — easy effort throughout, 148–155 HR. Last few miles of some long runs include marathon pace (MP) segments — noted when prescribed.
- **Cutback weeks** — marked with ↓. Reduce volume, keep intensity. Don't skip the tempo, just shorten it.

## Notes

- **Paces are HR-first.** If HR is creeping above the ceiling, slow down. Don't chase the pace number on easy days.
- **Tempo HR builds slightly** as the plan progresses — 162–165 early, 164–169 late. That's normal fitness adaptation.
- **MP segments** in Weeks 10 and 12 are checkpoints, not workouts. Run them honest. They'll tell you exactly where you are.
- **Hot weather adjustment** — Jun/Jul in DC will be humid. HR will run 5–8 BPM higher in heat. Slow down to stay in zone. Don't fight it.
- **This plan ends Jul 12.** Weeks 13–27 (Jul 13 – Nov 2) continue in Phase 3 → 4 → Taper.
